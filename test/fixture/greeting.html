What is it?

PageHub is an online text editor that makes writing technical documents a snap. It can also be used for keeping notes.

The pages you create on PageHub can be shared with people, groups of people, or be kept private to yourself.

Why?

Because it's hassle-free, and it's all about writing.

Pages are written in Markdown so your documentation can be rendered as man entries, or as fully-fledged HTML and PDF documents.

But that's only scratching at the surface; this page describes more of what PageHub offers.
